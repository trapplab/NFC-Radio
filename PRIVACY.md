# Privacy Policy
Last updated: 31.12.2025

NFC Radio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## 1. Data Collection

NFC Radio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

* No user accounts
* No analytics or tracking
* No advertising SDKs
* No crash reporting
* No backend servers

All app functionality operates entirely on the user’s device.

## 2. In-App Purchases (Pro Features)

NFC Radio offers optional Pro features that can be unlocked through Google Play In-App Billing.

All payments are processed by Google Play

The app does not collect or store payment information

The developer does not receive access to users’ Google account details, payment information, or personal data related to purchases

Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for information on how Google processes payment data.

## 3. Affiliate Links

The app may include affiliate links to third-party websites or products related to optional hardware or services.

* Affiliate links open external websites
* Any purchases or interactions occur outside the app
* NFC Radio does not receive personal data from these third parties
* The privacy practices of external websites are governed by their own privacy policies.

## 4. Internet Usage

Internet access is used only to open external links, such as affiliate links or documentation.

No user data is transmitted to the developer.

## 5. Third-Party Services

NFC Radio does not use third-party services that collect user data, including:

* Analytics services
* Advertising networks
* Marketing SDKs
* Crash reporting tools

## 6. Children’s Privacy

NFC Radio does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data is collected, no personal information is stored or shared.

## 7. Open Source Transparency

NFC Radio is an open-source application. The source code is publicly available and can be reviewed to verify how the app works and how data is handled.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
